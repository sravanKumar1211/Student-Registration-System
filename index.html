STUDENT REGISTRATION SYSTEM

The Student Registration System is designed to streamline the process of collecting, organizing, and managing student information in a structured and efficient manner. It allows institutions to register students by capturing essential details such as name, student ID, contact number, email, and address. The system helps reduce manual paperwork, minimizes errors, and ensures that student data is easily accessible and manageable.
